Hyper Security Privacy Policy

What information we gather and use

Our team respects your desire to protect personal information. This page tells you what sort of information we may ask you for, and in what situations, when using Hyper Security.

You make all decisions regarding whether or not to proceed with any activity that requests personal information. However, please note that if you do not wish to provide the requested information, you may not be able to receive Hyper Security ‘s protection.

What Information We Collect

1. Device information

When you use Hyper Security services, our servers automatically record certain information about your mobile device. This information may include an equipment identifier (e.g., IMEI, UDID), Mac address, subscriber identifier (e.g., IMSI), device name, mobile phone number, device type and manufacturer, operating system type and version, wireless carrier / operator, network type, country of origin, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and the dates and times of your requests. We use this information to provide our services, and support if needed, and to create de-identified or aggregate information about our users.

2. Information about general user behaviors

We use analytics software so that we can improve the features and usability of our products. This software might analyze information such as how often you use the application, the events that occur within the application, and where the application was downloaded. We may also use such information to show you relevant content and suggest new features, products and services that can enhance your user experience with Hyper Security. We do not link the information we store within the analytics software to any personally identifiable information you submit within the mobile app.

3. Location information

Some security features we offer work better if we know what area you are in. Hyper Security might collect location information in two ways. We may receive it directly from your mobile device or in some situations, we may receive location data from cell tower or Wi-Fi hotspot information.

4. Information about scans and scan results

Hyper Security features, which are available on different platforms, help protect your device from malware and spyware. They scan files and applications after you install Hyper Security and as new applications are installed or accessed to detect any threats to you and your mobile device. They also automatically scan your entire device periodically, and regularly update your threat definitions. In order to perform these functions, we need to collect information about the applications and files present on your device, the results of any scans performed by our services, and actions you take as a result of scanning.

5. Safe Browsing / URLs

Safe Browsing is a feature available when using the Android default browser, the Chrome browser and other supported browsers. Safe Browsing is designed to identify and warn you of unsafe websites so that you can choose to avoid visiting them. It scans URLs that you visit on supported browsers.

To provide the Safe Browsing service, we may transmit the URLs you receive or visit on your phone to a Hyper Security provided or third-party service to determine if those URLs are unsafe (e.g., if the URLs contain phishing attacks, malwares, or exploits). If a URL you visit is determined to be unsafe, we store a record of the unsafe URL.

6. Privacy issues related new features

Call Blocking requires to access manage your phone and contact list. Therefore, Hyper Security is able to block the numbers that users refuse to answer. Meanwhile, the app can block numbers from user’s contact list. We never collect or save numbers from our users.

When users send feedback to us, the email information will be attached. However, we won’t use the contact details for other purposes. These details only will be used when customer services.

In order to locate user’s issues quickly, user error log might be submitted. Again, non-personal information would be sent. All information we need is to fix the problem and therefore improve the app.

For some new features we are going to introduce in the near future, we are committed to protect our user privacy. We would never abuse users’ privacy. Protecting privacy is one of our top priorities. Please enjoy your protection without any worries.
